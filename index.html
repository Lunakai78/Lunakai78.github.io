Meet Our Adorable Characters!

Ducky

The mischievous but lovable duck who loves to play pranks! Ducky teaches children about friendship, consequences, and how to resolve conflicts with kindness. Though he seems naughty, he has a heart of gold!

Pup

The adorably naughty little puppy who's always getting into mischief while trying to protect his precious tail! Pup teaches kids that even good friends can be playful troublemakers and shows how to balance fun with responsibility.

Sotong

The clever underwater helper with amazing tentacles! Sotong uses her eight arms to solve tricky situations and teaches kids about teamwork and creative thinking.

Bele

The gentle giant with an incredible memory! Belephant helps friends remember important lessons and teaches patience, kindness, and the value of never giving up.

Ianz

Pup's most loyal follower and adventure buddy! This clever cat is always ready to join Pup's mischievous plans and teaches children about friendship, loyalty, and having each other's backs through thick and thin.

Zel

The surprisingly friendly shark who protects the group! Zel shows that appearances can be deceiving and teaches children about courage, loyalty, and not judging others.
